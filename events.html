Ideathon for First years

23rd and 24th Mar, 2024

Ideathon for first years was conducted as the continuation of the previous session on how to approach Hackathons and Ideathons, where the students were given 3 different tracks:
1. Challenges within MITS: Identifying the hurdles within our campus community and brainstorming innovative solutions to enhance campus life.
2. Public Transportation: Explore ways to enhance public transportation and make commuting more efficient and accessible for all.
3. Issues faced by women: Shine a spotlight on gender-related challenges and propose empowering solutions to promote equality and inclusivity.
There were a total of 3 teams, who were asked to submit their ideas and then pitch it before the judges.
